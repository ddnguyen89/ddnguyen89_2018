Introduction

Howdy, welcome to my website. I am currently a student at Western Governors University completing my Bachelors of Science in Software Development. Prior to Western Governors University, I had attended Coleman University in pursuit of the same degree. However, due to circumstances, Coleman University had made the decision to discontinue their operation August 5, 2018. I was able to receive my Associates of Science in Software Development from Coleman University but was 3 courses shy from a Bachelors Degree prior to the closure.

During this time, I had also been working as a sushi chef. It was a good means to financially support my pursuit for a higher education. Being a sushi chef throughout the years has taught me management, communication, and timing. However, the same year Coleman University came to a closure, I was ultimately let go in October 2018.

I spent a couple months, reevaluating my life's focus. I went through a number of different school interviews to find a school that would fit my goals and needs. I ultimately chose to go to Western Governors University as it was the most logical option financially. I am glad to say I am near the end of my goal and am now seeking a full time software development position.
